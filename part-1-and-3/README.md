# StockFlow Case Study  
## Part 1: System Understanding & Part 3: API Implementation

This directory contains **Part 1 and Part 3** of the StockFlow case study assignment. These sections focus on understanding business requirements and implementing an API endpoint based on those requirements.

---

## Part 1: System Understanding & Requirements Analysis

### Objective  
To analyze the StockFlow problem statement and identify the core business requirements for a B2B inventory management platform used by small businesses.

### Scope of Analysis  
The system was reviewed to understand how companies manage inventory across multiple warehouses while maintaining supplier relationships.

### Key Business Requirements Identified
- A company can operate multiple warehouses
- Products may exist in more than one warehouse with varying quantities
- Inventory changes must be tracked over time
- Suppliers provide products to companies
- Products may be standalone items or bundles composed of other products

### Outcome  
This analysis established a clear understanding of:
- Core entities and their relationships
- Data needed for inventory and supplier management
- Open questions that must be clarified before final system implementation
